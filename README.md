# Commercial Vehicle Demand & Expansion Analysis

Business focused analysis of commercial vehicle demand trends in India using Government transport datasets.

---

## Project Objective
This project aims to identify high-growth Indian markets for commercial vehicle dealership expansion using official Government transport datasets.

The goal is to transform raw registration data into actionable business insights for strategic expansion planning.

---

## Data Source
Ministry of Road Transport & Highways (India)  
Road Transport Year Book (2020–21 & 2021–22)

---

## Business Questions
- Which states show the highest commercial vehicle growth?
- What are the year-on-year demand trends?
- Which regions indicate strong expansion potential?
- How can data-driven insights support dealership expansion strategy?

---

## Project Phases

### Phase 1 – Completed
- Data collection  
- Data cleaning & preprocessing  

### Phase 2 – In Progress
- Trend analysis  
- Year-on-year growth calculation  
- High-demand state identification  
- Data visualization  

### Phase 3 – Planned
- Demand forecasting using regression models  
- Market clustering for expansion prioritization  
- Strategic recommendation model
